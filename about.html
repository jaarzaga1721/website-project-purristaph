ABOUT US

Purrista PH is designed to be a local cat café locator for Filipino students, young adults, and pet lovers. It helps users discover cat cafés near them, learn about adoptable cats, and find budget-friendly cat cafés.

The objective of the website is to provide a user-friendly platform for cat café enthusiasts — featuring details such as location, opening hours, and reviews. Additionally, the website promotes budget-friendly cat cafés for those who wish to enjoy coffee while spending time with adorable cats.
